ABOUT

Mirror Snake is a minimalist twist on the classic game of Snake. In Mirror Snake, every swipe defies logic. Go up, snake goes down. Go right, it goes left. Sounds easy? Just wait till you try Dual Mode — two snakes, one set of controls, total chaos.

HOW TO PLAY

• Single Mode: Snake moves in the opposite direction of your swipe.

• Dual Mode: Control two snakes at once, one with normal controls, one with mirrored controls.

PRIVACY

Mirror Snake is completely offline and does not collect or store any personal data. The game operates entirely on your device without requiring internet connectivity.

SUPPORT

Q: Does the game require internet connection?
No, Mirror Snake works completely offline.

Q: Is my data being collected?
No, we don't collect any personal data. The game runs locally on your device.

Q: How do I reset the game?
Tap the reset button during a game or start a new game from the menu.

Q: Is there a time limit?
Yes, each game has a three-minute time limit to keep matches quick and exciting.
